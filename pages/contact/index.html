Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our contact and support processes.

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our customer service line for immediate assistance.

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form and selecting "Service Quote" from the subject dropdown, or by calling our sales team directly. Please provide details about your shipment size, destination, and timeframe for an accurate quote.

Our offices are open on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Our online support team is available for urgent issues on Sundays via email or through our mobile app's chat feature.

You can view current job openings and submit your application through our Careers page. Alternatively, you can email your resume to careers@kglexpress.ng with the position you're interested in as the subject line.
