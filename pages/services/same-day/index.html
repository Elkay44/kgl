Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our same-day delivery service

For same-day delivery, we require all requests to be placed before 2:00 PM. Pickups scheduled before 12:00 PM will be delivered by 6:00 PM the same day. Pickups scheduled between 12:00 PM and 2:00 PM will be delivered by 8:00 PM.

We cover all major areas within Kano metropolis including Kano Municipal, Nasarawa GRA, Bompai, Gyadi-Gyadi, Sabon Gari, Fagge, Hotoro, Sharada, Kumbotso, Rijiyar Zaki, Tarauni, and along Kano-Zaria Road. Contact us to confirm if your specific location is within our same-day delivery zone.

For safety and legal reasons, we cannot transport the following items: hazardous materials, flammable substances, illegal items, weapons, live animals, perishable food items requiring refrigeration, and any item prohibited by law. If you're unsure about an item, please contact us before booking.

Once your package is picked up, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to track your delivery in real-time. You can also track your delivery by logging into your account on our website or mobile app. For additional assistance, you can contact our customer service team.
