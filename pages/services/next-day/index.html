Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our next-day delivery service

For next-day delivery, we require all requests to be placed before 4:00 PM on the day before the desired delivery date. Packages picked up after 4:00 PM will be processed the next business day for delivery on the following business day.

We offer next-day delivery to all major cities and towns across Nigeria. This includes all state capitals and major commercial centers. For remote or less accessible areas, delivery may take an additional 1-2 business days. You can check our coverage areas above or contact our customer service for specific location information.

While we strive to deliver all packages on time, certain circumstances beyond our control (such as weather conditions, road closures, or recipient unavailability) may cause delays. If your package is not delivered by the guaranteed time, please contact our customer service immediately. We'll investigate the delay and, if the delay was due to our error, we'll provide appropriate compensation as per our service guarantee policy.
