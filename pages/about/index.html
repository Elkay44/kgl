At KGL Express, we're on a mission to transform logistics in Nigeria by providing fast, secure, and reliable delivery services that connect businesses and individuals across the nation.

Our Journey

The Road We've Traveled

From humble beginnings to becoming Kano's leading delivery service, our journey has been defined by continuous growth and innovation.

2015

Founded in Kano

KGL Express was founded by Ibrahim Musa with just three delivery bikes and a small office in Kano. Our first clients were local businesses needing same-day document delivery.

2017

Expanded to E-commerce

As online shopping grew in Nigeria, we expanded our services to include e-commerce deliveries, partnering with major online retailers to provide last-mile delivery services.

2019

Opened Regional Hubs

We established regional distribution centers in Abuja and Lagos, expanding our coverage and reducing delivery times across Northern Nigeria.

2021

Technology Transformation

Launched our proprietary logistics platform with real-time tracking, automated dispatching, and a customer mobile app, revolutionizing our operations and customer experience.

2023

Sustainability Initiative

Introduced our eco-friendly delivery fleet, including electric bikes and solar-powered warehouses, as part of our commitment to reducing our carbon footprint.

2025

Nationwide Expansion

Today, we're expanding our services nationwide, with plans to cover all 36 states by the end of the year, making KGL a truly national delivery brand.
